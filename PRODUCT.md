# PRODUCT.md

register: brand

## What this is

Maged Faiz's personal portfolio (v2 → "V3" redesign milestone). Not a freelance/agency site. The product is proof of work: long-form, first-person case studies grounded in real code, with real snippets linked back to their repos.

## Thesis

Proof-of-work in the AI era. A slick site is cheap to fake now; judgment is not. The site's job is to show how Maged thinks while he ships: decisions, dead ends, trade-offs. The centerpiece feature is interactive annotated code: hover a token in a snippet and get an authored note, in two kinds ("decision": why this, what I rejected; "context": what it does).

## Users

- Hiring managers and senior engineers deciding whether to interview or work with Maged. They skim the home page, then read one case study properly.
- Peers and friends who read the case studies as essays.
- Future Maged, coming back a year later to remember why the code is the way it is.

## Voice

Story-driven, first-person, honest about mistakes, lightly funny, never salesy. The case studies read like a good engineering blog, not marketing. Copy rules: straight apostrophes, no em dashes.

## Strategic principles

- Writing is data (Portable Text in Sanity); code is first-class content (Shiki-highlighted snippets with filenames and source links).
- Strip the freelance/agency layer: no services grid, no FAQ, no contact form, no tool-icon walls, no agency copy. Contact is an email link.
- Every claim on the site must be backed by real code. No invented metrics, no fake logos, no testimonial carousels.
- Stripping must not mean bland. The design has to carry personality on typography, color commitment, and motion, because the content layer got quieter.

## Anti-references

- Generic dark developer portfolio: near-black, neon accent, tool-icon wall, "I build scalable web apps" hero.
- Freelancer template sites with services/pricing/FAQ sections.
- AI-slop landing pages: centered hero stack, icon-title-text card grids, gradient text.

## Current identity (v2, being redesigned)

Dark near-black (#010001), periwinkle accent (#96b7e3), Space Grotesk, side-rail navigation, content down the middle. Treat as a starting point, not a constraint; one redesign variant may evolve it, another may depart entirely.
